Seek ye First: How honoring God first with your finances is the best investment you can make

Posted by Admin / Lighthouse Media
March 15 ,2016

Practiced by both Christians, Hebrews and some other religions, the first fruit is a religious offering of an increase in a way of life or harvest. Our constitution the bible, talks about the first fruit offering in Deuteronomy 26, how, where and whom to give this offering to. It also distinguishes between tithing and the first fruit offering. Tithing is an act of giving a tenth of our income to the house of the Lord. The bible in Malachi 3:10 commands “Bring the whole tithe into the Storehouse, that there may be food in my house…”
