Welcome to RCCG Lighthouse, Mansfield. Tx

We are awesomely delighted to have you visit our website! Lighthouse for all nations is one of the parishes of the Redeemed Christian church of God. We hope that this site will offer you an opportunity to explore refreshing resources that will enhance your faith.

At the Lighthouse for all Nations, you will discover friendly, multi-cultural environment where you can enjoy heart lifting music and relevant messages that addresses everyday concerns. You are cordially invited to our physical location to experience God’s presence and more of His radiating love.
